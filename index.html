About me

Building systems that actually hold up

I am a Senior Software Engineer with 7+ years of experience building production-grade backend systems, cloud-native infrastructure, and data pipelines. My career has spanned federal health agencies, aerospace engineering, fintech, and energy tech.

Currently building Pawgo — a Toronto-based dog walking platform connecting pet owners with trusted, insured walkers via real-time GPS tracking and in-app booking.
